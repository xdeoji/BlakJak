# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

BlakJak ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated by Deoji. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## What We Collect

### Analytics
We collect anonymous gameplay data to understand how the app is used. This includes:
- Hands played, skipped, and outcomes
- Bet amounts and chip balances
- Session duration
- App version
- Streak activity

This data is collected through PostHog and is not linked to your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.

### iCloud
We use iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync your chip balance across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is controlled by Apple. We do not have access to your Apple ID or iCloud credentials.

### In-App Purchases
Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store payment information.

---

## What We Do Not Collect

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Your location
- Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA)
- Any data from your contacts, camera, microphone, or photos

## Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. iCloud data persists until you delete the app or sign out of iCloud.

---

## Children

BlakJak is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of any analytics data associated with your device by contacting us. Because analytics are anonymous, we cannot guarantee identification of your specific data.
